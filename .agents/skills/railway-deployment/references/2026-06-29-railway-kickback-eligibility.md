# Railway Kickback Eligibility — Source Document

Source: https://docs.railway.com/templates/kickbacks (fetched 2026-06-29)

## Eligibility Requirements (exact from docs)

1. **Marketplace:** Your template must be published to the template marketplace. Private and unpublished templates are not eligible.
2. **Terms of Service:** Your project must abide by Railway's Fair Use Policy and Terms of Service. Templates that violate Railway's Terms of Service may be removed and kickback payments deemed ineligible.

**No mention of certification, verification, or approval anywhere in these requirements.**

## Kickback Rates

- 15% of usage costs incurred by users deploying your template
- Support Bonus: Additional 10% (25% total) when actively supporting users via your Template Queue

## How Earnings Are Calculated

"If a user pays $20 in platform fees, then incurs $200 of usage from your template, you are eligible for up to a $50 kickback (25% of $200 for an open source template with active support)."

Minimum payout: $0.01

## Withdrawals

- Default: Automatic conversion to Railway Credits
- Cash withdrawals available via Stripe Connect ($100-$10,000 increments)
- Supported in 130+ countries (not Brazil, China, Russia)

## Open Source Partner Program (separate from kickbacks)

Source: https://docs.railway.com/templates/partners

This is a separate opt-in program. Requirements:
1. Open source or open-core project
2. Apply at railway.com/partners
3. Onboarding: template verification → documentation → social promotion

**What verified templates get:**
- Verified template badge
- Featured placement
- Product management (template queue, metrics)
- Monetization through commission & private Docker images

**Technology Partnership (for technologies with community-created templates):**
- Commission on all templates using the technology (even ones the creator didn't make)
- Dedicated technology page on Railway
- Extended co-marketing
- Maintainer listing on community templates

## Key Takeaway

Kickbacks (15-25%) are automatic for ANY published template on the marketplace. The Verified/Partner program is OPTIONAL and purely additive — it adds a badge, featured placement, and extra perks but is NOT a gate for kickbacks.
