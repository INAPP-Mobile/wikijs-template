## What Happens After Submission

Railway's team reviews the application and reaches out. Accepted applicants get a partner thread for onboarding guidance.

## Onboarding Steps (from docs)

### Open Source Partnership:
1. Template verification by Railway (against best practices)
2. Documentation: standalone guide for deploying on Railway
3. Social promotion after verification

### Technology Partnership:
1. Select templates featuring your technology
2. Railway verifies a selection as "recommended"
3. Partner profile + dedicated page
4. Co-marketing

## Key Distinction

Being a partner is OPTIONAL for kickbacks. The verified badge is an ADDITIONAL perk, not a requirement for earning commission. Any published template gets the base 15% kickback automatically.
